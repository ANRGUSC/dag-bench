SplitStream Video Query Pipeline

ID: edge.splitstream_pipeline  |  Domains: edge-computing, ml-pipeline  |  Completeness: structure-and-costs  |  Cost Model: deterministic

Distributed video query pipeline for edge-based traffic analytics, faithfully extracted from Figure 1 of Liang et al. (JNCA 2024). An object detector (YOLOv5m) classifies detected objects as person or vehicle, routing to two cascaded classification branches: person branch (gender classifier → behaviour classifier) and vehicle branch (type classifier → maker classifier), with results collected at a final aggregation node. Deployed across heterogeneous edge devices (Jetson Nano, AGX Xavier, Intel E5 server).
