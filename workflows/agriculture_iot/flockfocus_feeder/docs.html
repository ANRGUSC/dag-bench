FlockFocus Feeder Monitoring Pipeline

ID: agri.flockfocus_feeder  |  Domains: agriculture-iot, ml-pipeline  |  Completeness: structure-and-costs  |  Cost Model: deterministic

Video analytics pipeline for automated poultry feeding behavior monitoring, faithfully extracted from Figure 1 of Ghafouri et al. (arXiv 2025). The feeder pipeline detects chickens (UNeXt) and feeders (YOLOX) in parallel, tracks chickens over time via multi-object tracking, analyzes feeding behavior based on spatial proximity to feeders, and estimates bird weights from bounding box dimensions. Deployed on AWS g4dn.2xlarge instances with NVIDIA T4 GPU.

Notes: DAG structure faithfully extracted from the Feeder pipeline row of Figure 1 ('FlockFocus system architecture and per-zone video pipelines'). The feeder pipeline monitors chicken feeding behavior at commercial poultry farms. Tasks are the processing modules with sharp-cornered boxes in the figure: ChickenDetector (UNeXt segmentation), FeederDetector (YOLOX detection), TrackingAlg (two-stage tracking-by-detection), FeedingAnalysis (feeding behavior classification based on proximity to feeder lines), WeightAnalysis (regression model for weight estimation from bounding box dimensions). Task costs are proportional to processing times reported in the paper: detection ~2000s, tracking ~5000s, behavior analysis ~22000s per 5-minute video on AWS g4dn.2xlarge (NVIDIA T4, 16GB VRAM, 8 vCPUs). Data transfer sizes are estimated from intermediate result dimensions. Affiliations: Queen's University Belfast.
